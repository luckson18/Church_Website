About Us

Our History

Grace Fellowship Church was founded in 1999 by Pastor David and Mary Johnson in a small living room with just twelve families. Driven by a vision of radical hospitality and biblical community, the church grew steadily over the years.

In 2007, we moved into our current worship centre on Maple Avenue, and by 2015 we launched our second campus across town. Today, we are a multi-generational congregation with ministries spanning youth, seniors, women, men, and community outreach.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love people, and serve the world.

Our Vision

A church where every person encounters the transforming love of God and finds their God-given purpose.

Our Values

Faith · Family · Community · Integrity · Generosity · Excellence in Worship
